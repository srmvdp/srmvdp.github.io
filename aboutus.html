About Us!

Welcome to SRM VDP Papers. This website is developed by Ritesh Hota, Sruthik P. and Raghav Hari Krishna of the Computer Science and Engineering Department of SRM University, Vadapalani (2018). We hope to provide a means of communication and resource sharing between faculty and students, outside of the classroom.

Students will find all the important notices over here. Srm Vdp Papers has segments covering the needs and interests of all students. The website contains records of the academic experiences of different students in different domains, projects etc. The website contains all the previous year question papers of all departments year wise. It even contains notes prepared by faculty and well prepared hand written notes by students.
